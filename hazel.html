About Me

-min

I am Hazel, a digitalhobochic. I am from the Philippines, a lovely country in South East Asia. Life isn’t easy for me especially when I was growing up. I practice being a minimalist and it worked for me. To be happy and contented even if I only have little. And crave for more memories and experience instead. That’s why now it is my immediate response to say “no” to unnecessary expenses. It has become like a reflex over the years and that is what keeps me going in life. Being frugal. Finding hacks and affordable alternatives into buying or doing things that I wanted.



Like in travel. That is one of my guilty pleasures. I came from a least waged income household. But this did not stopped me from travelling to 17 countries and counting in 3 years. I research the most affordable fun activities to do in a country, couchsurf anytime possible, booking tickets only when its on sale and use travel points, travel with friends and share expenses, I can go on with more tips but I’ll save it for a travel blog. What I am trying to say is, getting into something involves right mindset, focus and strategy.



I studied engineering but never graduated. That leaves me to work on low paying jobs available to me when I was just starting. I gained different work experience over the years. I was once a food server then worked in an amusement park and to working in call centers. And in the past in 3 years, I work online, freelancing and as an affiliate marketer. That’s how I am able to finance my travels and little pleasure in life today. I teach myself stuff entrepreneurs needed in their businesses. Like coding and affiliate marketing. And it served me okay. But I wanted to do more.
